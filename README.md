## Features

### Billing & Invoicing
- GST-compliant tax invoices
- Automatic GST calculation (CGST, SGST, IGST)
- Custom invoice templates with business branding

### Digital Khata (Ledger)

- Credit / debit tracking
- Outstanding balance calculation

### GST & Compliance

- Financial year-wise reports

- Sales & purchase reports
- Profit & loss summary

- Daily / monthly business insights
